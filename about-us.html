Work Process

We work with a wide variety of raw materials. We are able to translate DIN, British, JIS grades into required specific grades. Our list of raw material is as follows.

PLATES, STEEL ROUND BARS 01

  • All kind of flame cut materials
  • All types of structural steel grades
  • Case Hardening Steel, Steel for nitriding
  • S235JR, S235JRH , P235, E295, E335, S355JR, IS Standard plates, Boiler Grade Plates.

CASTING 02

All kinds of castings: steel iron, ductile iron (nodular), grey iron, precision castings, aluminium castings, bronze castings, brass castings. Specially GGG 40, GG 20, GG 15.

NON FERROUS METAL 03

All kinds of aluminium-alloys Typically: AlMg3, AlMgMn, AlZnMgCu, AlCuMgPb, AlZnMgCu1,5, AlZnMgCu0,5,Al 6082, Al 6061, Al 6063 Different kinds of copper-alloys, typically: E-Cu 57, CuZrCr, CuBe2 Different kinds of bronzes and “red castings”, typically: CuZnPb, RG 7 and bearing-metal Brass in blocks, in castings and as sheet material
